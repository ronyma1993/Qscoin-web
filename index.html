What is QSCOIN?

QSCOIN is a next-generation cryptocurrency designed to remain secure in a world with quantum computers. We use post-quantum cryptography (PQC) to protect keys, transactions, and wallets against quantum attacks, keeping your assets safe for the long term.

🔒 Quantum-Safe

Hybrid signature schemes (PQC + classical) to ensure security today and tomorrow.

⚡ Fast & Scalable

Lean design targeting low fees and fast confirmations without sacrificing security.

🌍 Community-Driven

Transparent roadmap, open development, and inclusive governance over time.
