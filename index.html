What is QSCOIN?

QSCOIN is a next-generation cryptocurrency designed to remain secure in a world with quantum computers. We use post-quantum cryptography (PQC) together with classical primitives (hybrid signatures), zk-rollups for scalability & optional privacy, and a PoS + VRF consensus.
