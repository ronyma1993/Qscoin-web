Abstract

QSCOIN is a next-generation, quantum-safe cryptocurrency designed to remain secure in the advent of quantum computing. By combining post-quantum cryptography (PQC), hybrid consensus, and zero-knowledge proofs, QSCOIN aims to deliver long-term resilience, scalability, and privacy.

Vision

Our vision is to build a digital currency that is not only resistant to current threats but also future-proof against the capabilities of quantum computers, ensuring safety and trust for decades to come.
